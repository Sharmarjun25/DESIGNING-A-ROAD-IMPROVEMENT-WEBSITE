Road Transportation

Our city features an extensive network of well-maintained roads and highways, ensuring smooth and efficient road travel. Public buses and taxis are readily available for daily commuters, while dedicated lanes for bicycles promote eco-friendly travel.

Metro Transportation

The metro system connects all major parts of the city, offering a fast, safe, and affordable mode of transportation. Modern trains and frequent services make the metro a reliable option for all residents.
